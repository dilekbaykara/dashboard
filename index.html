Alert: Cat missing in Brooklyn

Location: Bay Ridge

A female Turkish Van cat (age 6) with blue/green eyes has disappeared from her residence on Wednesday evening, all cats are advised to report to their owners if she is seen. She wears a distinctive teal sparkly collar with bells. Please call 1800-cat-lost for more information.

Cat stolen from bodega in Park Slope returns

A mysterious intermediary returned the feline to the Green Olives Deli & Grill in Park Slope a week after a catnapper grabbed the gray kitty outside the Seventh Avenue shop. Abdulmajeed Albahri, one of three owners of the bodega, said he was reunited with Boka at 5 a.m. Saturday when he arrived an hour before the store opened for the day.
